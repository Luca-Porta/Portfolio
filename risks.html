Online risks

The technology is going to be more and more important by passing years, but it can be dangerous if we use it too much in the day.
We splitted three tipes of technology and we found some common problems with people who use them too much.

Risk and dangers of Videogames:

  • Violence and aggression: connection between videogames and aggressive behaviour
  • Health and sedentary: physical effect of exessive gaming
  • Dependence: the uncapacity of stop gaming


Risk and dangers of Social Media:

  • Cyberbullying: make joke about people online
  • Privacy: the security of personal data
  • Mental health: influence of social on self-esteem


Risk and dangers of Smartphones:

  • Distracted driving: using phone while we driving
  • Social disconnection: paradox between smartphones and real life
  • Screen addiction: consequences of exessive screen time
